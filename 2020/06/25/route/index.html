feedback from James

  1. Be careful of articles(a/an/the).
  2. Many people make mistakes when answering why questions. We will talk about this many times on the course. A simple answer is WHAT YOU DID + BECAUSE + WHY YOU DID IT. Eg. I have applied to study architeture because I have always been interested in buildings and design.
  3. You should also be careful using the verb to help. In English this has a specific meaning,eg. Can you help me move this table? Student often use help in sentences when it is better to use useful, necessary or enable.

1.1 present simple and continuous

present simple
this include:
(1) describing regular activities
(2) describing processes or giving instructions
(3) summarizing or reporting the main arguments of other academics
(4) talking about the plots of books, films, etc

present continuous
(1) describing events or actions that are happening at the moment
(2) temporary situations and trends in society or the world around us

tips:
state verbs such as believe, know, mean; like, prefer, want; belong, own, possess; contain, depend, and matter are usually used in the present simple.

1.2 past simple and continuous

(1) The past simple is used, often with dates, times or places, for finished events, whether they are long, short, or repeated.

2020.6.26

present perfect(and past)

When a past time marker is not mentioned, the present perfect can be used to connect the past with the present by doing the following three things:

1) describing accepted current theories/studies established in the past
2) summarising a current argument made in the past
3) describing a present effect of a past action
